Who Should Choose a Soft Mattress?

Side Sleepers

Side sleeping creates pressure points at shoulders and hips. Soft mattresses cushion these areas, preventing pain and maintaining alignment.

Lightweight Individuals

Under 130 lbs? Firmer mattresses may feel like sleeping on a board. Soft mattresses allow proper sinking for comfort and support.

Pressure Pain Sufferers

Soft mattresses distribute weight more evenly, reducing pressure on joints and muscles for those with arthritis or fibromyalgia.

Cloud-Like Feel Preference

Some people simply prefer the sensation of sinking into their mattress. Soft mattresses provide that plush, enveloping comfort.

Who Should Avoid Soft Mattresses?

  • Stomach sleepers: Need firmer support to prevent hip sinking and lower back strain
  • Heavier individuals (over 230 lbs): May sink too deep, losing support and causing alignment issues
  • Back pain sufferers: Often need medium-firm for proper lumbar support (though some benefit from soft)
  • Hot sleepers: Soft mattresses often retain more heat due to deep sinking into foam
