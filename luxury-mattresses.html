Is a Luxury Mattress Worth It?

Consider a luxury mattress if:

  • You have specific sleep issues like chronic pain, temperature regulation problems, or sleep apnea
  • You weigh over 230 lbs and need extra support and durability
  • You plan to keep the mattress 10+ years and want maximum durability
  • You sleep hot and need advanced cooling technology
  • You value white glove service and professional setup

If you're a healthy sleeper without specific needs, a mid-range mattress ($500-$1,000) may provide similar sleep quality at better value.
