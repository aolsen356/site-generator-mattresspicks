What to Expect at This Price Point

Good Enough Comfort

Budget mattresses provide solid comfort for most sleepers. Don't expect luxury, but expect adequate support and pressure relief.

Shorter Lifespan

Lower-density foams may compress faster. Expect 5-7 years vs. 8-10 for premium mattresses.

Basic Features

You won't get advanced cooling, zoned support, or premium covers. Focus on core support quality.

Limited Trials

Many budget mattresses have shorter return windows or no trials. Check policies before buying.

Budget Mattress Buying Tips

Prioritize Support Over Features

At this price, focus on solid support and adequate foam thickness. Skip models with thin comfort layers. A 10-12" mattress typically offers more value than an 8" version.

Check the Warranty

Even budget mattresses should have at least a 10-year warranty. This indicates the manufacturer stands behind their product's durability.

Read Recent Reviews

Amazon reviews can reveal quality control issues or recent changes to materials. Focus on verified purchases from the last 6-12 months.
