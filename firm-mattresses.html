Who Needs a Firm Mattress?

Stomach Sleepers

Firm mattresses prevent hip sinking that causes lower back strain. Essential for comfortable stomach sleeping.

Heavier Individuals (230+ lbs)

More body weight requires more support. Firm mattresses prevent excessive sinking and maintain alignment.

Back Sleepers with Pain

Some back pain sufferers need extra lumbar support that firm mattresses provide better than soft options.

Those Who Sleep Hot

Firm mattresses allow less body contact with the surface, improving airflow and reducing heat retention.
