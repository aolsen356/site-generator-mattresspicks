Why Medium-Firm is the Most Popular

Works for Most Sleepers

Medium-firm accommodates back, side, and combination sleepers within the 130-230 lb weight range, making it ideal for couples with different preferences.

Spinal Alignment

Research suggests medium-firm mattresses provide optimal spinal support for most people, reducing back pain and promoting healthy sleep posture.

Balance of Comfort & Support

You get enough cushioning for pressure points without sacrificing the support needed to maintain proper alignment.

Safe Default Choice

If you're unsure what firmness you need, medium-firm is the safest bet. Most people can adjust to it comfortably.

Medium-Firm Buying Tips

Consider Your Body Weight

Medium-firm works best for average-weight sleepers (130-230 lbs). If you're under 130 lbs, medium-firm may feel too firm. If you're over 230 lbs, it may feel too soft. Adjust your choice accordingly.

Hybrid vs. All-Foam

Hybrid: Better for hot sleepers, those who need edge support, and couples who want less motion transfer. All-Foam: Better for tight budgets and those who want deeper contouring.

Use the Trial Period

Give your new mattress at least 30 days before deciding. Your body needs time to adjust to a new firmness level. Most online mattresses offer 90-365 night trials for this reason.
