Memory Foam Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Excellent pressure relief for joints
  • Superior motion isolation for couples
  • Contours to body shape
  • Quiet, no squeaking
  • Generally more affordable
  • Hypoallergenic

Cons

  • Can trap heat
  • Slow response, may feel "stuck"
  • Initial off-gassing smell
  • Weaker edge support
  • Harder to move on/change positions
  • Some feel "quicksand" sensation
