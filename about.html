About MattressPicks

We're dedicated to helping you find the perfect mattress for your unique sleep needs.

Our Mission

Buying a mattress is a significant investment in your health and well-being. With so many options available online, the decision can feel overwhelming. MattressPicks exists to cut through the noise and provide clear, research-backed recommendations organized by the factors that matter most: your sleep position, firmness preference, and budget.

We believe everyone deserves a great night's sleep, regardless of their budget. That's why we review mattresses across all price points, from budget-friendly options under $500 to luxury mattresses over $1,000.

How We Research

Comprehensive Analysis

We analyze construction details, materials, certifications, and manufacturing quality for every mattress we review.

Customer Feedback

We aggregate and analyze thousands of verified customer reviews to identify patterns in comfort, durability, and satisfaction.

Expert Insights

We consult sleep science research and chiropractic recommendations to understand what makes a mattress supportive.

Value Assessment

We evaluate whether each mattress delivers fair value for its price point, considering materials, warranty, and trial period.

What We Look For

When evaluating mattresses, we consider factors including:

  • Sleep Position Suitability: How well the mattress supports different sleeping positions
  • Firmness Level: Accurate firmness ratings on a 1-10 scale
  • Pressure Relief: How effectively the mattress relieves pressure on joints
  • Motion Isolation: How well it minimizes partner disturbance
  • Temperature Regulation: Cooling features and breathability
  • Edge Support: Stability when sitting or sleeping near the edge
  • Durability: Expected lifespan based on materials and construction
  • Trial Period & Warranty: Return policy flexibility and long-term coverage
  • Value: Quality relative to price point

Our Review Philosophy

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all mattress recommendations. The "best" mattress depends entirely on your individual needs:

  • A side sleeper needs different support than a stomach sleeper
  • A lightweight person experiences firmness differently than a heavier person
  • Hot sleepers need better cooling than those who sleep cold
  • Couples have different needs than solo sleepers

That's why we organize our recommendations by specific categories, making it easier for you to find mattresses suited to your particular situation.
