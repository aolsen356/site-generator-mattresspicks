Best Hybrid Mattresses (2024)

Hybrid mattresses combine foam comfort layers with an innerspring or pocketed coil support core. This construction delivers the best of both worlds: pressure-relieving foam on top with responsive, breathable coil support underneath. They're ideal for sleepers who want foam comfort without the heat retention or "stuck" feeling of all-foam mattresses.

Why Choose a Hybrid?

Better Cooling

Coils allow airflow through the mattress that all-foam designs can't match. Essential for hot sleepers.

Stronger Edge Support

Reinforced coil perimeters provide stability for sitting on edges and maximize usable sleep surface.

Responsive Feel

Coils bounce back quickly, making it easier to change positions and get in/out of bed.

Balanced Support

Foam contours to pressure points while coils provide core support and prevent excessive sinking.
